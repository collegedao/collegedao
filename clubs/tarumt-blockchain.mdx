# About

The TARUMT Blockchain Club is a student-led organization at Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology dedicated to exploring blockchain technology. The club organizes activities to educate students on the fundamentals of distributed ledger technology and the Web3 ecosystem [instagram.com](https://www.instagram.com/tarumtbcc/?hl=en).
