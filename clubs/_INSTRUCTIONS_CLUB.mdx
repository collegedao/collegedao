# Club Template Instructions

## Frontmatter

Fill in the three required fields:
- `name`: Your club's official name
- `university`: Your institution's name
- `region`: Choose one of: North America, South America, Europe, Middle East, Asia, Oceania, Africa

## About

Replace the placeholder paragraph with a short bio (2-3 sentences) describing your club's mission and activities.

## Socials

1. Keep the badges for platforms you use; delete the lines for ones you don't.
2. Update the URL inside the parentheses `(https://______)` to link to your specific profile.
3. Do not modify the long image link inside the brackets `[...]`.

## Wallets

Add your club's public wallet addresses. This is required to vote and receive funding.

## Donations

Update the link path to match your club's slug (e.g., `donations/clubs/your-club-slug/`).

## Totals

Leave as default. These will be updated automatically as contributions are recorded.

## Contacts

List the primary point of contact(s) for the club with their name, telegram handle (optional), and email (university email preferred).
