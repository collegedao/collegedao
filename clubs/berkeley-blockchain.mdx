# About

Blockchain at Berkeley is a university-based organization dedicated to serving the crypto and blockchain communities. Our members comprise of 60+ active undergraduate and graduate students from Berkeley Engineering, Haas, and Berkeley Law. We drive innovation in the blockchain industry by building an ecosystem that empowers students to make an impact through practical education, consulting for enterprise companies, and conducting open source research.
