# About

The NUS Blockchain Club operates as the specialized Blockchain Department within the NUS FinTech Society, the leading student-led Fintech club at the National University of Singapore. The group aims to build a thriving community around the latest technology developments in distributed ledgers and their applications in finance. They actively engage in projects involving smart contracts, dApps, and quantitative finance, while organizing workshops and hackathons to educate students and connect them with industry partners. [fintechsociety.comp.nus.edu.sg](https://fintechsociety.comp.nus.edu.sg/)
