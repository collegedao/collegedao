# About

The Sunway Blockchain Club (SBCC) is a student-led initiative at Sunway University dedicated to igniting curiosity and nurturing the next generation of blockchain innovators. Affiliated with Sunway iLabs, the club serves as a gateway to the Web3 space, offering workshops, seminars, hackathons, and social events for students to learn and network [sunwayblockchain.com](https://sunwayblockchain.com/en). The organization aims to empower members from all academic backgrounds to explore job opportunities and collaborate on blockchain technology [innovationlabs.sunway.edu.my](https://innovationlabs.sunway.edu.my/education/).
