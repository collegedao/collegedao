# About

Founded in 2017, Blockchain@NTU is a non-profit student club of blockchain enthusiasts with backgrounds ranging from computer science, math, and business at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Blockchain@NTU is dedicated to fostering a vibrant blockchain community in NTU and beyond, committed to building and strengthening the technical expertise and market awareness of members while empowering like-minded individuals in pursuit of their interests in academia, industry, or entrepreneurship.
