# About

The Blockchain at Emory is a student-led club dedicated to exploring everything related to blockchain technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between the complex fintech industry and our members by fostering a deeper understanding of blockchain. Through engaging lesson plans, talks with industry experts, and networking events, we equip our members with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate the ever-evolving landscape of the blockchain industry. The interest group is a dynamic platform for students passionate about blockchain, providing valuable insights and preparing them for future opportunities in this transformative space.
