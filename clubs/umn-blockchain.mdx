# About

The University of Minnesota Blockchain Club welcomes all students to join and get involved in the ever-evolving crypto space. Whether you are new to Web3, experienced, or skeptical, our club offers the best environment to learn and explore career opportunities in the field through presentations, speakers, conferences, and hackathons.
