# About

Taylor’s Blockchain Club is a student-led organization at the Taylor's University Lakeside Campus dedicated to fostering a community of blockchain enthusiasts. As part of Taylor's vibrant student life ecosystem, the club aims to provide a platform for students to explore distributed ledger technologies, network with industry professionals, and participate in collaborative projects [college.taylors.edu.my](https://college.taylors.edu.my/en/get-in-touch/contact-information/social-media-directory.html).
